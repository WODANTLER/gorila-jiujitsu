Who We Are

More Than a Gym.
A Grappling Community.

We believe that the mat is the ultimate equalizer. It doesn't matter if you're a complete beginner or a seasoned competitor — We are passionate about grappling in all formats and we have a passion for teaching and building the next generation of grapplers.

Located in the south of Mallorca, our academy brings competitive-level BJJ to the island.
